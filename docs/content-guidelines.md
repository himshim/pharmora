# 📚 Pharmora Content Guidelines

## Building reliable open pharmacy knowledge

These guidelines help maintain quality, accuracy and usefulness of educational resources shared on Pharmora.

---

# 🌱 Content Philosophy

Pharmora content should be:

- Accurate
- Easy to understand
- Well organized
- Educational
- Continuously improved

The goal is not collecting files.

The goal is building structured pharmacy knowledge.

---

# Accepted Contributions


## 📝 Study Notes

Recommended format:

- Clear topic title
- Learning objectives
- Important definitions
- Concept explanation
- Diagrams where useful
- Examples
- Summary points


Good notes should help students understand, not only memorize.

---

# 🖥 Presentations

Recommended:

- Clean slides
- Topic focused
- Easy classroom explanation
- Correct terminology
- Proper references when needed


Avoid:

- Excessive text
- Unverified information
- Poor formatting

---

# ❓ MCQs & Questions

Should include:

- Question
- Options
- Correct answer
- Explanation (preferred)
- Topic mapping

Subject:
Pharmacology
