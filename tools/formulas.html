Pharmaceutical Formula Sheet

Important calculations used in pharmacy.

Dilution Equation

C₁V₁ = C₂V₂

Percentage Strength (% w/v)

(Weight of solute ÷ Volume) × 100

Dose Calculation

Dose = Body Weight × Dose/kg

BMI

Weight(kg) ÷ Height²(m)

Density

Density = Mass ÷ Volume
