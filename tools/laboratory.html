Laboratory Calculations

Common calculations used in pharmacy laboratories.

Molarity

Moles of solute ÷ Volume of solution (L)

Normality

Gram equivalents ÷ Volume of solution (L)

Percentage Error

(Observed - True value) ÷ True value × 100

Serial Dilution

Stepwise dilution of solution concentration.

Parts Per Million (PPM)

mg of solute ÷ L of solution
