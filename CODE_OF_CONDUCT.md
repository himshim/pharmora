# ⚕️ Pharmora Code of Conduct

## Our Mission

Pharmora exists to build an open, collaborative, and accessible pharmacy learning ecosystem.

We welcome students, educators, developers, researchers, and healthcare professionals working together to improve pharmacy education.

---

# 🌱 Our Values

Members of the Pharmora community are expected to promote:

- Open sharing of knowledge
- Respectful discussions
- Scientific thinking
- Collaboration
- Inclusiveness
- Constructive feedback

---

# ✅ Expected Behaviour

Community members should:

- Be respectful and professional
- Help others learn
- Share knowledge responsibly
- Welcome new contributors
- Give constructive suggestions
- Respect different experience levels
- Credit sources and contributors

Examples:

✔ Helping someone understand a concept  
✔ Improving existing resources  
✔ Reporting mistakes politely  
✔ Sharing better explanations  

---

# ❌ Unacceptable Behaviour

The following are not acceptable:

- Harassment or personal attacks
- Disrespectful language
- Spam or misleading content
- Sharing knowingly incorrect information
- Plagiarism
- Uploading unauthorized copyrighted material
- Disrupting discussions

---

# 📚 Educational Responsibility

Pharmora content should aim to be:

- Accurate
- Evidence-based
- Clearly explained
- Continuously improved

If you find an error:

Do not criticize the contributor.

Help improve the knowledge.

---

# 👨‍🏫 Educators & Contributors

Teachers and experienced contributors are encouraged to:

- Guide learners
- Review resources
- Improve explanations
- Share experience

Recognition should come through contribution and collaboration.

---

# 🛡 Moderation

Maintainers may:

- Review contributions
- Remove inappropriate content
- Request improvements
- Protect community quality

Actions are taken to maintain a healthy learning environment.

---

# 🌐 Open Community

Everyone contributing positively to pharmacy education is welcome.

Together we build:

**The future of pharmacy learning — open, collaborative, and accessible.**

---

⚕️ Thank you for being part of Pharmora.
