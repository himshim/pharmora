# Pharmora Vision

> **Knowledge should never be limited by where someone studies, works, or lives.**

## Why Pharmora Exists

Pharmacy knowledge is one of humanity's most valuable resources, yet much of it remains scattered across books, lecture notes, research papers, institutional repositories, websites, personal collections, and isolated communities.

Every day, students prepare excellent notes, educators create outstanding teaching materials, researchers publish important discoveries, pharmacists gain practical experience, and professionals develop valuable resources. Most of this knowledge is difficult to discover, inaccessible to many learners, or eventually lost.

Many talented people possess knowledge, skills, and experience but have no open platform where they can contribute, collaborate, and continuously improve together.

Pharmora exists to change that.

Our mission is to build an open, structured, community-driven platform where pharmacy knowledge can be shared, connected, verified, preserved, and continuously expanded by the global community.

# What Makes Pharmora Different

Pharmora is not simply another educational website, note-sharing platform, research repository, or community forum.

It is an open knowledge ecosystem.

Instead of treating information as isolated files or disconnected pages, Pharmora connects knowledge through structured relationships.

A lecture connects to a subject.

A subject connects to a curriculum.

A drug connects to its class, mechanism, indications, interactions, guidelines, and research.

A research paper connects to references, organizations, authors, and scientific evidence.

Knowledge becomes a living network instead of isolated documents.

# What We Believe

Every piece of pharmacy knowledge should be openly available whenever possible and connected to all related knowledge.

Knowledge should never exist in isolation.

Its value increases when ideas, evidence, people, organizations, and learning resources become connected.

---

# Open Source Philosophy

Pharmora Core is built as an open, modular, community-driven platform.

We welcome contributions from students, educators, researchers, pharmacists, developers, organizations, and institutions.

Open source enables transparency, collaboration, and continuous improvement.

Cloud services may enhance the platform, but learning should never depend entirely on proprietary infrastructure or vendor lock-in.

---

# Sustainability

Keeping Pharmora sustainable is essential for its long-term future.

Professional services, institutional integrations, premium capabilities, donations, sponsorships, or other ethical funding models may help support development.

However, sustainability should never compromise the project's mission of expanding access to pharmacy knowledge.

People should always remain free to learn, contribute, and grow with the community.

---

# What We Will Never Become

Pharmora will never intentionally become:

* A closed ecosystem.
* A platform built around vendor lock-in.
* A marketplace that restricts learning through unnecessary barriers.
* A service that sells personal user data.
* A project that values growth over trust, openness, and quality.

Technology should empower people—not control them.
