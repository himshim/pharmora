# ⚕️ Pharmora

## The future of pharmacy learning — open, collaborative, and accessible.

Pharmora is an open-source pharmacy education ecosystem designed to bring pharmacy students, educators, researchers, and professionals together through shared knowledge and collaboration.

The goal is to create a modern, community-driven platform where pharmacy education resources are easy to access, improve, and share.

---

## 🌐 Live Preview

https://himshim.github.io/pharmora/

---

## 🌱 Vision

Pharmora believes that pharmacy knowledge should be:

- **Open** — freely accessible educational resources
- **Collaborative** — built by students, teachers, and professionals together
- **Accessible** — available through a fast, lightweight, mobile-friendly platform

---

# ✨ Platform Modules


## 🎓 Pharmora Learn

A structured learning space for pharmacy courses.

Planned support:

- B.Pharm
- D.Pharm
- M.Pharm
- Pharm.D


Features:

- Curriculum-based organization
- PCI syllabus support
- NEP curriculum ready
- Semester/year organization
- Subject resources
- MCQs and quizzes
- Exam preparation


---

## 👨‍🏫 Pharmora Teach

A dedicated space for educators.

Includes:

- Lecture resources
- Teaching strategies
- Presentation materials
- Course files
- Academic templates
- Topic explanation methods


Goal:

Help teachers teach better and share academic experience.


---

## 🌐 Pharmora Community

A collaborative pharmacy discussion network.

Features:

- Doubt discussions
- Verified explanations
- Resource suggestions
- Academic discussions
- Contributor reputation

## 🧪 Pharmora Tools
