> **The Open Pharmacy Knowledge Platform**

Pharmora is an open-source, community-driven platform that brings together pharmacy education, research, professional collaboration, scientific knowledge, and learning resources into one connected ecosystem.

Our goal is simple:

> **Make pharmacy knowledge open, connected, collaborative, and accessible to everyone.**

# рџЊЌ Why Pharmora?

Pharmacy knowledge is often scattered across books, journals, lecture notes, institutions, websites, and personal collections.

Pharmora brings these resources together into one structured platform where students, educators, researchers, pharmacists, organizations, and developers can learn, contribute, and collaborate.

Whether you're looking for lecture notes, research papers, books, SOPs, calculators, jobs, practicals, or community discussions, Pharmora aims to become the central destination for everything related to pharmacy.
